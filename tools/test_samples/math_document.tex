\subsection{Inline Mathematics}

The function $f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!} = e^x$ is the exponential function.
We have $\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\sin x}{x} = 1$ and $\int_0^1 x^2 dx = \frac{1}{3}$.
